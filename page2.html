Characters Description

Crisostomo Ibarra

  • He is a young wise man who returned to the Philippines from studying in Europe.
  • He represents the desire of the Filipino people for freedom, equality, and justice during the Spanish colonial era.

Maria Clara

  • She is portrayed as a symbol of pureness, innocence, and the standard Filipina woman in her time.
  • Her father is Capitan Tiago and she is deeply in love with Crisostomo Ibarra.

Padre Damaso

  • He is portrayed as a Spanish Friar and his position is within the Catholic Church in the Philippines.
  • He has an abusive figure and is abusing his power.

Sisa

  • Sisa is a mother of two children named Crispin and Basilio.
  • She is a caring and loving mother, she symbolizes the suffering endured by the Filipinos under the cruel Spanish colonial regime.
